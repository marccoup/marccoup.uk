If you've followed my blog for any length of time (doubtful!) you'll notice my entire backlog of (6) posts have disappeared from the site. This is simply because I have not ported them to the new version of this site.

<!--more-->

Part of me misses them, but they weren't works of art. Only 2 of them were real blog posts with anything to say anyway (even that is debatable), the others were variations of (I have rebuilt the site again, with new bad design!). **THIS TIME** it will be different. This site is pretty well set up, probably due to the fact I used a [Statamic starter kit](https://statamic.com/starter-kits/statamic/stumblr), and spent a bunch of time browsing the indie web while setting it all up, which has me more inspired to just put stuff out there!

There's something here about the value of starting fresh and letting go of the past as well. Like, I thought to myself when I ditched those old posts "Oh people will think I only just set up this site/blog/whatever" - but really, should I care? Does that matter? No it doesn't. I guess I didn't really get over that either since I wrote this post. Oh well, in the words of Eleanor Shellstrop "Pobody's Nerfect" (I'm sure that phrase came before then but I don't care to do the research*).
